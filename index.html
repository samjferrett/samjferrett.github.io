ABOUT ME

I am currently a Climate Scientist. I have a Ph.D. in Mathematics and have been working in climate data analysis for around ten years. My research interests include:

  • Representation of the tropical climate and weather in coupled climate models and forecast models.
  • Uncertainty in future changes in climate.
  • Dynamics and teleconnections of modes of variability (e.g. equatorial waves, El Nino-Southern Oscillation etc.) in the Tropical Pacific.

RECENT PROJECTS

WCSSP SE Asia: FORSEA

Analysis of the skill of high resolution forecasts of rainfall in Southeast Asia and construction of hybrid dynamical-statistical forecasts of heavy rainfall conditioned on equatorial wave and weather regime activity

  • Construction of hybrid forecasts
  • Skill of high resolution forecasts

CIRCULATES: Cloud Feedbacks

Examining and constraining modelled cloud feedbacks in deep convective atmospheric regimes.

  • Deep cloud feedbacks in CMIP6: In Review
